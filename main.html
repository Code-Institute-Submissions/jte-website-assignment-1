Journey to excellence

Founded in 2014, JTE Recruit is a professional and dynamic recruitment firm providing people solutions and effective Human Resource strategies in Singapore. Boutique in size, we punch far above our weight. JTE Recruit was build on the strength of its talent acquisition expertise, servicing corporate clients through a team of professional and highly dedicated recruiters. As a firm that strongly believes in quality and progress, JTE Recruit seeks to constantly improve and outperform our competitors to deliver excellence. Over at JTE, we believe in building relationships with our clients and keeping their interests at heart. Through our highly personalised services, we have connected our clients with the right talents who will bring their businesses to greater heights of success.

Our services

JTE Recruit works on established networks, contacts and know-how of serving you and meeting your recruitment needs.

With years of experience in the Human Resource field across various industries and hiring market at large, we are confident of delivering maximized results, We have our core values and principles which serve as guidance in the way we cultivate our relationships and find success in our careers, as well as in building your business.

JTE Recruit focuses on an in-depth understanding of your business. We go beyond and handle complex “wall street” work with a difference. We do not believe in having you to tailor your Human Resource needs according to our practice units. Our people specialize across practice domains to curate “Big Picture” personalised solutions as seamlessly as possible. It is this experience we undertake to tailor-made recruitment solutions for your organization. No matter how far your organization has moved, we aim to bring your talent pool further.

At JTE Recruit, you will find yourself business partners instead of mere recruiters. We are equipped with market insights, in depth knowledge of employment trends. We recruit hard and we recruit fair. We take ownership of our roles to ensure long-term success through achieved results and progressive enhancements.

You will have the undivided attention of a team.
