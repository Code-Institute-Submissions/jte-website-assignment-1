Journey to excellence

Started in the mid of 2014.. 2 brave entrepreneur noticed the lack of flexiblity, adaptivity and transparency of employment agencies in the market. Decided to take a risk and co-founded a professional and dynamic recruitment firm providing people solutions and effective Human Resource strategies in Singapore. JTE are full of young, vibrant, creative and innovative consultants. We equipped ourselves with the latest news, knowledge and skills together with the abilities to adapt and move as one accordingly to the needs of our clients. With our unique approach JTE constantly has a competitive edge over other agencies. Through our highly personalised services, we have connected our clients with the right talents who will bring their businesses to greater heights of success.
